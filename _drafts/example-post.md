## Adding a post

Drop a Markdown file in `_posts/` named `YYYY-MM-DD-your-title.md` with front matter, then write in Markdown:

```markdown
---
layout: post
title: "Your title"
date: 2026-06-15
excerpt: "One-line summary that shows on the blog index."
---

Your post body, in **Markdown**.
```

It'll appear automatically on the [Blog](/blog/) page and get its own page at `/blog/your-title/`.
